Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ Do’s
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments  
- Verify information before sharing it online  
- Maintain privacy settings to protect personal data  
- Share positive and professional content  

❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information like phone number or address  
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links  
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Avoid spreading fake news or rumors  
- Do not misuse social media platforms or engage in cyberbullying

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone  
- Always check the website URL before entering personal information  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Use only trusted and official apps for payments and transactions  
- Regularly update your phone and apps for security  
- Be cautious of calls pretending to be from banks or officials

  

UPI / Financial Safety Tips

- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps  
- Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money
